The Four General Patterns of Universe

The General Patterns of Universe are a set of guidelines for approaching everything from your daily routine, to learning a new skill or concept, to designing something from scratch, and everything inbetween.

What's really interesting is that these patterns hold true on more than just one level of reality. They exist everywhere in the physical, tangible world. They pervade the conceptual and cognitive realms of the mind. They are at the roots of spirituality, philosophy, morality, and other "soul-level" phenomena. Not too bad for four simple ideas.
